## Partner Integration Roadmap for Itinerary-Guard

### Target Integrations

| Partner/API                       | Free Tier Limits           | Integration Effort | Value-Add (User Job Solved)                                       | Rationale for Selection                                       |
|-----------------------------------|----------------------------|--------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Skyscanner API**                | 1000 requests/month        | M                  | Provides flight and hotel data for itinerary verification          | High user base; affiliate program available                  |
| **Amadeus Travel API**            | 1000 requests/month        | L                  | Access to comprehensive travel data including flights, hotels, and car rentals | Established in the travel industry; revenue-sharing options  |
| **Google Places API**             | 100,000 requests/month     | S                  | Validates location data and provides user reviews                 | High accuracy and trust; enhances itinerary validation       |
| **TripIt API**                    | 200 requests/month         | M                  | Integrates user itineraries for real-time verification             | Popular among frequent travelers; potential affiliate revenue |
| **Kayak API**                     | 200 requests/month         | M                  | Offers travel search capabilities for flights, hotels, and car rentals | Strong brand presence; affiliate program available           |
| **OpenWeatherMap API**            | 60 calls/minute            | S                  | Provides real-time weather data to validate travel conditions      | Enhances user experience; critical for travel planning       |
| **FlightAware API**               | 500 requests/month         | M                  | Real-time flight tracking and status updates                       | Essential for itinerary accuracy; potential revenue share    |
| **Booking.com API**               | 100 requests/day           | L                  | Access to hotel availability and pricing for itinerary validation   | Large inventory; affiliate program can drive revenue         |

### Summary

The integrations listed above will enhance the functionality of Itinerary-Guard by providing accurate, real-time data that users can rely on before booking their travel. Each partner has been selected based on their market presence, potential for affiliate revenue, and the specific user jobs they help solve, ensuring a comprehensive and valuable service offering.
