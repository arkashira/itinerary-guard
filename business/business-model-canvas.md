# Business Model Canvas for Itinerary-Guard

## Customer Segments
- **Frequent Travelers**: Individuals traveling for business or leisure, estimated at 1.4 billion globally.
- **Travel Agencies**: Agencies managing itineraries for clients, with a market size of approximately $300 billion.
- **Corporate Travel Managers**: Companies spending an average of $1,000 per employee on travel annually.
- **Airlines and Hospitality Providers**: Businesses seeking to enhance customer experience and reduce booking errors.

## Value Propositions
- **Real-Time Verification**: Cross-checks itineraries against multiple authoritative sources to ensure accuracy.
- **Error Prevention**: Flags inconsistencies before booking, reducing the risk of costly mistakes.
- **Time-Saving**: Automates the verification process, allowing users to focus on travel planning.
- **Enhanced Trust**: Builds confidence in travel arrangements, leading to increased customer satisfaction.

## Channels
- **Website and Mobile App**: Direct access for users to verify itineraries.
- **API Integrations**: Partnerships with travel agencies and platforms for seamless integration.
- **Social Media Marketing**: Engaging content to attract travelers and agencies on platforms like Instagram and Facebook.
- **Email Campaigns**: Targeted outreach to corporate travel managers and agencies.

## Customer Relationships
- **Self-Service Platform**: Users can independently verify itineraries through the app.
- **Customer Support**: Dedicated support team available via chat and email for assistance.
- **Community Engagement**: Online forums and feedback loops to gather user insights and improve services.
- **Loyalty Programs**: Incentives for frequent users, such as discounts on future services.

## Revenue Streams
- **Subscription Model**: Monthly fee of $19.99 (approx. 700 THB) for individual users.
- **Tiered Pricing for Agencies**: Starting at $199/month (approx. 7,000 THB) for agencies with up to 50 clients.
- **API Access Fees**: Charge per API call for partners integrating the service, estimated at $0.10 per call.
- **Premium Features**: Additional charges for advanced analytics and reporting tools.

## Key Resources
- **Technology Infrastructure**: Robust backend systems for real-time data processing and verification.
- **Data Partnerships**: Agreements with authoritative travel data sources for accurate information.
- **Development Team**: Skilled engineers and data scientists to maintain and enhance the platform.
- **Marketing Team**: Experts in digital marketing to drive user acquisition and engagement.

## Key Activities
- **Data Verification Processes**: Continuous development of algorithms to cross-check travel data.
- **User Experience Optimization**: Regular updates to the app based on user feedback.
- **Marketing Campaigns**: Ongoing efforts to promote the service to target segments.
- **Partnership Development**: Establishing relationships with travel agencies and data providers.

## Key Partners
- **Travel Data Providers**: Companies offering reliable travel information (e.g., airlines, hotels).
- **Travel Agencies**: Collaborations to integrate itinerary verification into their services.
- **Technology Partners**: Cloud service providers for hosting and data storage solutions.
- **Marketing Affiliates**: Influencers and bloggers in the travel industry to promote the service.

## Cost Structure
- **Technology Development**: Estimated at $200,000 annually for software development and maintenance.
- **Marketing Expenses**: Approximately $50,000 annually for digital marketing campaigns.
- **Customer Support Costs**: Around $30,000 annually for staffing and operational expenses.
- **Data Acquisition Costs**: Estimated $100,000 annually for partnerships with data providers.
