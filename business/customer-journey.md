# Customer Journey Map for Itinerary-Guard

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: User realizes the importance of accurate travel information after experiencing a booking error or itinerary inconsistency.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about existing solutions; overwhelming amount of travel information sources.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, anxiety about future travel plans, desire for reliability.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content on the importance of itinerary verification; engaging social media campaigns highlighting common travel pitfalls.
- **Metric**: Number of users reached through awareness campaigns (e.g., website visits, social media engagement).

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: User begins researching solutions to ensure travel data accuracy after recognizing potential risks.
- **Friction Points**: Confusion over the variety of services; difficulty comparing features and benefits of different solutions.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful yet cautious; desire for reassurance and clarity.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Clear comparison charts, testimonials from satisfied users, free trials to test the service.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from website visitors to sign-ups for trials or demos.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: User signs up for a free trial or demo of Itinerary-Guard.
- **Friction Points**: Complexity of the onboarding process; potential technical issues during initial use.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with apprehension; eagerness to see the service in action.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Streamlined onboarding experience; personalized support during the trial period; interactive tutorials.
- **Metric**: User engagement rate during the trial (e.g., frequency of use, features explored).

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: User successfully verifies an itinerary using Itinerary-Guard and experiences peace of mind.
- **Friction Points**: Initial subscription cost; potential concerns about ongoing value.
- **User Emotions**: Relief, satisfaction, and increased trust in the service.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Highlighting successful verification stories; offering loyalty discounts for long-term subscriptions; proactive customer support.
- **Metric**: Subscription renewal rate and customer satisfaction scores.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: User considers additional features or services offered by Itinerary-Guard after positive experiences.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about the value of additional features; potential information overload.
- **User Emotions**: Curiosity, excitement about new possibilities, but also hesitation.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Tailored recommendations based on user behavior; exclusive access to new features for loyal customers; educational webinars on maximizing service benefits.
- **Metric**: Upsell conversion rate for additional features or services.
