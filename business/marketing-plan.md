# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
Itinerary-Guard is a real-time travel-info verification service that ensures your travel plans are accurate and reliable by cross-checking itineraries against multiple authoritative sources before booking.

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**  
1. "Travel with confidence: Itinerary-Guard verifies your travel plans in real-time, eliminating booking errors."
2. "Never miss a detail: Itinerary-Guard cross-checks your itinerary against trusted sources for peace of mind."
3. "Stay ahead of travel disruptions: Itinerary-Guard flags inconsistencies in your travel plans before they become issues."

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Sarah the Corporate Travel Manager**  
   - **Daily Job:** Manages travel arrangements for executives and employees, ensuring cost-effectiveness and compliance with company policies.  
   - **Budget:** $5,000/month for travel management tools and services.

2. **Persona 2: John the Frequent Business Traveler**  
   - **Daily Job:** Travels weekly for client meetings and conferences, requiring accurate and timely travel information.  
   - **Budget:** $500/month for personal travel tools and services.

3. **Persona 3: Emily the Travel Agency Owner**  
   - **Daily Job:** Operates a travel agency that books travel for clients, needing reliable data to provide the best service.  
   - **Budget:** $10,000/month for agency tools and services.

## Channels
1. **LinkedIn Advertising**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $150 per acquired customer. Targeting corporate travel managers and agency owners.

2. **Content Marketing (Blog & SEO)**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $100 per acquired customer. Focus on travel accuracy, tips, and industry insights to attract organic traffic.

3. **Partnerships with Travel Agencies**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $200 per acquired customer. Collaborate with agencies to integrate Itinerary-Guard into their offerings.

- **Week 3:** Webinar on "How to Avoid Travel Booking Mistakes" + email marketing to ICP.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features and user interface; begin beta testing with select users.
- **D-0:** Official product launch; initiate marketing campaigns across selected channels.
- **D+30:** Collect user feedback; iterate on product features; begin targeted outreach to travel agencies.
- **D+90:** Evaluate marketing effectiveness; adjust strategies based on performance metrics; plan for next phase of product enhancements.

## Success Metrics
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Target 500 DAU by D+90.
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue ($MRR):** Target $50,000 MRR by D+90.
