# 🚀 itinerary-guard

**Power travelers and agencies with real-time entry restriction checks.**

Automate compliance validation against official government advisory feeds to prevent costly booking errors and ensure regulatory adherence before departure.

## Why itinerary-guard?

- **Compliance First**: Auto-validates itineraries against official government advisory feeds to ensure legal entry.
- **Risk Reduction**: Flags inconsistencies before booking, reducing the risk of costly mistakes and denied boarding.
